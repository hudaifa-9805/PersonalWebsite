About Me

The Story Behind
the Resume

I'm a Finance student with a focus in FinTech at UT Dallas, with real experience building and shipping products across AI startups and Big 4 consulting. I've owned end-to-end product execution — writing PRDs, defining success metrics, managing backlogs, and keeping engineers and stakeholders aligned from discovery through launch.

I'm passionate about FinTech because financial products have some of the highest potential to create real user impact, and I genuinely enjoy being the person who bridges technical teams with that bigger picture. Whether I'm triaging dataflow issues, refining requirements with engineers, or turning research into a product recommendation — I bring both the analytical foundation and the communication skills that great PM work actually requires.

What makes my background a little different is that I'm a former CS major with over 10 years of coding experience who made a deliberate choice to move into Finance with a FinTech focus. I didn't want to just build things — I wanted to build the right things in an industry I care about. That means I can get into the weeds with engineers on implementation while staying locked in on the business and user outcomes that matter most.
